console.chat [beta]

What is this?

You're going to think I'm crazy but it's exactly what it sounds like. A chat room inside your browser's console.

No way!

Yes way! Open your console right now to see a demo.

Implementation

Add this to your site's HTML on every page you want console.chat activated (no more steps required):

<script src="https://console.chat/load.js"></script>
